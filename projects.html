Projects

Throughout my academic career, I have worked and developed multiple projects, each showcasing my passion for innovation and problem-solving. LostAndFound, Nome, and Crypto Gamer are just a few examples of the diverse range of projects I've undertaken.

From facilitating lost item recovery to providing precise rhythm control for musicians, and even creating engaging platforms for cryptocurrency gaming, my projects are designed to enrich user experiences through innovation and utility.

Explore the creativity and functionality embedded within each project, and discover how they can enhance various aspects of daily life.

LostAndFound

LostAndFound is a web application that allows users to search for their lost items for public use. Users can make a post of a lost item, write a brief description, pin on the map where the item was last seen, and display the amount of reward if the item is found.

Nome

Nome is an Android metronome application developed using Kotlin. It provides users a constant and exact beat or rhythm in music, allowing them to keep a constant pace while performing or studying music, preparing for an acting role, and more.

Crypto Gamer

Crypto Gamer is a web application that allows users to wager cryptocurrency while playing different games. Players can play with others and set bets on various games including Word Search, Color Guessing, and Tic-Tac-Toe. The amount of cryptocurrency won or lost depends on the player's performance in these games.
